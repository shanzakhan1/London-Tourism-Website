Popular London Foods

Fish and Chips

Fish and chips is a classic British dish consisting of battered fish, usually cod or haddock, served with deep-fried potato chips. It's a popular and iconic meal enjoyed by locals and tourists alike.

Learn more

English Breakfast

The English breakfast is a hearty meal traditionally served in the morning. It typically includes eggs, bacon, sausages, baked beans, grilled tomatoes, mushrooms, and toast. It's a substantial and delicious way to start the day.

Learn more

Cornish Pasty

The Cornish pasty is a savoury pastry filled with meat, potatoes, and vegetables. It originated in Cornwall and has become a popular grab-and-go meal for locals and tourists.

Learn more

Afternoon Tea

Afternoon tea is a British tradition that involves a selection of teas, sandwiches, scones with clotted cream and jam, and various sweet treats like cakes and pastries. It's a delightful and refined way to enjoy tea time.

Learn more

Shepherd's Pie

Shepherd's pie is a comforting and hearty dish made with minced meat, usually lamb, topped with mashed potatoes and baked to perfection. It's a classic comfort food loved by many.
